Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1/4 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 teaspoon vegetable oil
 - 1/4 cup chopped onion

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease 9-inch round frying pan.
 
 2. In a medium bowl, add eggs, salt and onions. Mix well along.
 	Pour the mix into heated frying pan.
 
 3. Leave it 2 minutes or until the liquid sops flowing. 
 	Flip the omelate to cook other side for 1 min.

 4. Its ready to be served.
